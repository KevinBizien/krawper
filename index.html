I am an artist and developer who spends most of his time creating animated and playable 2D characters and dynamic environments on different game engines.

The primary goal of most independent developers is to be able to create their own dream game, but my goal is to help you realize yours by providing you with quality assets offering a wide range of possibilities.

My main tools are the Unity game engine and the Visual Studio code editor, as well as the Aseprite editor to create my pixel art.

When you start developing your own game, you quickly realize the colossal task that awaits you.

I started developing my own games several years ago now, and I followed a lot of video tutorials and trainings in game design.
To practice, I notably recreated many games with mechanics similar to those I wanted to see in my future games.

For example, here are two of them : Axiom Verge and Super Dangerous Dungeons

Axiom Verge, remaked on Unity.
Originally developed solo by Thomas Happ on MonoGame.

Super Dangerous Dungeons, remaked on Unity.
Originally developed solo by Jussi Simpanen on Stencyl.

To achieve this, I redesigned the visuals pixel by pixel, and reimagined all the scripts based on the gameplay of the games.
I have not reproduced these games entirely, but it is an exercise that has taken me months of work and learning.

These copies have of course never been marketed or shared, it's just a way for me to practice.

My main fascination since I started developing games is the creation of the playable character.

Making its sprites, creating its animations, adding its sound effects, configuring its script to be able to control it,...
All this gives me the feeling of giving it life, and I'm really passionate about it !
